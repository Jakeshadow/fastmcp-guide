Privacy Policy

We keep it simple. No tracking, no ads, no funny business. Last updated June 13, 2026.

TL;DR

We don't track you. We don't have accounts, cookies, or analytics. When you buy something, Paddle (our payment processor) handles the transaction — we only see your email to deliver your purchase. That's pretty much it.

1. What We Collect

Almost nothing. This is a static site — no accounts, no logins, no comment sections, no cookies we set ourselves. We don't have analytics scripts. We don't track individual visitors.

When you make a purchase, Paddle (our payment processor) collects the information needed to complete the transaction — your name, email, payment method, and billing address. We receive your email and transaction confirmation. We never see your full payment details.

2. Third-Party Services

  • Cloudflare — Hosts and delivers the site. Cloudflare processes technical data (IP address, browser type) as part of serving pages and DDoS protection. Cloudflare's privacy policy.
  • Google Fonts — We load fonts from Google Fonts. Google may log the request. We don't use Google Analytics or any other Google tracking. Google's privacy policy.
  • Paddle — Handles purchases and payment processing. Paddle collects and stores payment information per PCI-DSS standards. We receive your email and order confirmation only. Paddle's privacy policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your email is used for exactly two things: delivering your purchase and responding to your support requests. We don't send newsletters, marketing emails, or promotional offers unless you've explicitly asked us to. We don't sell, share, or trade your information with anyone.

4. Data Retention

We keep purchase records (email + transaction ID) as long as needed for customer support and legal obligations. Want your data deleted? Email us and we'll take care of it — no questions asked.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete any personal data we hold. Just email us. Since we barely hold any data, this is usually pretty straightforward.

6. Contact

Privacy questions or data requests? privacy@deploymcp.dev. We read every email.
